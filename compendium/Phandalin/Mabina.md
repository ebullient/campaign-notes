## History

She has some kind of friendship or kinship with Harbin from an event that happened long ago. Because of that event, Harbin trusts trading magic items with her. 

The party traded "The Splendiferous Bauble" to her for the Handy Haversack. She was able to trade it again herself, and was quite pleased to do so (though she would not tell the party what she obtained in return). She will identify items for the party free of charge as a result.

> They kept talking, and Mabina asked them if they knew anything for sure about whether or not there was a dragon in the area. She used magic to hide herself, her cart, and her mule from would-be attackers, but a dragon might require some extra effort. Kyle was able to confirm that it was a dragon, and further, it was a young white dragon, as the party had a not so successful encounter with it already. 

> In return, Mabina warned of strange magic in Neverwinter Wood. She avoided entering the forest, and not only because she was in a wagon pulled by a mule. Navigators tended to get lost in the forest, even skilled navigators. Most humans stayed by the river, because it was harder to get lost that way. While she never met him, she heard that there was a noble from Neverwinter that had a Hunting Lodge in the woods along the river’s edge. Someone in town probably knew more than that, as the woods aren’t that far away.

> After thinking a bit, Kyle decided that, as a dealer in magical artifacts, she might be hiding something a bit rarer in her wagon. It was a risk, but he brought out the Splendiferous Bauble. Mabina was fooled by it. She took it and, as compelled by the bauble, went into the wagon to retrieve the most valuable item she carried, a rare Handy Haversack. Kyle took the backpack and the party took their leave of Mabina, who resumed fussing about her cart as she prepared to leave town.
