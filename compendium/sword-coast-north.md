# Sword Coast North

The Sword Coast North was a region on the northwestern coast of Faerûn.

It has a rough landscape of rugged hills, precarious mountain ranges and dense forests, 
and is home to several like-minded towns and cities. The most prominent of these cities 
banded together with nearby allies to form the [Lords' Alliance](groups/lords-alliance.md).
