# Silverymoon
<span class="subhead">The gem of the North, Confederacy of Silver Marches</span>

[Silverymoon](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Silverymoon) was often dubbed "the Gem of the North", for it resembled and emulated the (formerly) lost elven city of Myth Drannor in many respects. It is one of the few civilized places in the middle of the rough and untamed wilderness that is the North of Faerûn. 

* **Population** 40,000. Humans, Elves, Half-elves, Shield dwarves, Lightfoot halfling, Gnome
* **Government** High Lady Alustriel Silverhand

### Travel Distances from Silverymoon

* To Yartar - 260 miles (via the Evermoor Way)
* To Triboar - 320 miles (via the Evermoor Way)
* To Waterdeep - 612 miles (via the Evermoor Way and the Long Road)
