## History

Thel was captured and held by the Redbrands, and almost sold into slavery.

From the [Redbrand Hideout](/gm-story/encounters/redbrand-hideout.md): 

> Thel was brought here a few days ago, beat up, collared and dumped in the cell to be sold. He thought that was the end of it, but it was worse when his wife and children were brought in. 
>
> Thankfully, they suffered no abuse. Glasstaff came back from an outing of his own, and was annoyed with the Redbrands for taking an entire local family. Thel had to agree: taking a local family did attract attention, as here you all are.
>
> They called the goblin, "Droop". I have no idea if that is the right name or not. Droop ran all of their errands for them. The Bugbears stayed away from the rest of the RedBrands, which meant they pretty much stayed in that room. For whatever reason, they didn't pay us (the humans) any attention and heaped all of their abuse on poor Droop. While I'm sorry for Droop, I am thankful that they left me and my family alone.
