# Falcon the Hunter
![Falcon](/[assets]/attachments/falcon.png#portrait)
*Male human noble, {{alignment}}*  
*[Falcon's Hunting Lodge](Falcon's%20Hunting%20Lodge.md)*  

He wears a fur-lined cloak over his studded leather armor.

He stands 6 feet, 6 inches tall, and has black hair and broad shoulders. His eyes are as blue, cold, and sharp as ice, and he sports a neatly trimmed beard. 

He loves good wine and treats other people as he would like to be treated — fairly and with patience.

## Details
*Noble*, Commoner

Falcon abhors city life, preferring a rustic existence and simple pleasures. Falcon’s Lodge (Neverwinter Wood) has all the creature comforts he requires, though he never turns down a good bottle of wine (or even a bad one) from a visitor.

Falcon moves with the casual confidence of one who fears nothing, and he greets every concern with nonplussed indifference.

## History
