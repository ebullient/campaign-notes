# Grannoc (dead)
<span class="subhead">[Woodland Manse](../neverwinter-wood/woodland-manse.md), Neverwinter Wood</span>

Grannoc hoped Talos will answer his prayers and rain destruction down upon Falcon’s Hunting Lodge.

> *Male half-orc [Anchorite of Talos](/compendium/statblocks/anchorite-of-talos.md), neutral evil*
> * **Trait** I get bitter if I'm not the center of attention.
> * **Ideals** If I become strong, I can take what I want--what I deserve.
> * **Bonds** Something important was taken from me, and I aim to steal it back.
> * **Flaws** I am inflexible in my thinking.


## Details

He resided at Woodland Manse (Neverwinter Wood).

He planted the Gulthias tree in the well when he took over the Woodland Manse. The blights created by the tree obeyed him, and he intended to use them to control the denizens of the Wood.

He took in orcs fleeing from Cryovain. The stayed in the Manse, and helped with patrolling the Wood, and terrorizing Falcon and his Lodge.

Additional anchorites also stayed at the lodge and in the surrounding woods.

## Relationships

**Faction** Anchorite in the [Cult of Talos](/compendium/groups/cult-of-talos.md).

Took direction from / sent reports to High Priestess [Fheralai Stormsworn](/compendium/fheralai-stormsworn.md)

## Secrets

Grannoc wanted Falcon's Lodge as his base of operations. While he also bred blights and partnered with Orcs to gain control over the Wood generally, what he really wanted was for Falcon to be forced out of the Lodge so he could live there.
