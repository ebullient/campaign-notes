# Manticores
![manticore](/[assets]/attachments/manticore.jpeg#portrait)

Manticores living in the Sword Mountains were disturbed by the arrival of Cryovain.

One was killed while harassing [Adabra Gwynn](../Adabra%20Gwynn.md) in her home at [Umbrage Hill](../Umbrage%20Hill.md). Its mate has been seen several times since then.
