# Manticores
<span class="subhead">![manticore](/[assets]/attachments/manticore.jpeg#portrait)</span>

Manticores living in the Sword Mountains were disturbed by the arrival of Cryovain.

One, **Chanu**,  was killed while harassing [Adabra Gwynn](../Adabra%20Gwynn.md) in her home at [Umbrage Hill](../Umbrage%20Hill.md). His mate, **Zoolte**, has been seen several times since then.

## Notes

Zoolte attacked the party a few times, after they killed her mate.

The most recent time, they told her the dragon was dead. She didn't believe them, and bravely went to find out. The meat was still fresh enough for her taste, so she had a fine meal.
