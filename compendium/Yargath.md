# Yargath
![Anchorite](/[assets]/attachments/anchorite-of-talos.png#portrait)*Male half-orc Anchorite of Talos, neutral evil*  
*[Circle of Thunder](Circle%20of%20Thunder.md)*  

Yargath is tall, and mean looking, with several missing teeth. He has shoulder-length, thin dark hair that is held out of his face by a thin leather band. He uses flowery speech and long words. 

He is ready to bring the wrath of the storm lord to bear, and to wipe out the weak and non-believers who have been moving into the area.
