# High Road
*Trade road, North Sword Coast*

This highway hugs the coast, connecting Neverwinter to the coastal cities of Luskan to the north and Waterdeep to the south. 

For years, the stretch of road south of Neverwinter fell into disuse because of frequent monster attacks. 

Recently, efforts have been made to keep the road safe, with light patrols of guards on horseback moving between Neverwinter and Leilon.
