# The Cult of Talos

Talos, also known as the Lord of Storms, is a chaotic evil greater deity of storms, forest fires, earthquakes, tornadoes and destruction.
His followers are destroyers, raiders, looters and brigands. To ancient civilizations (the Netherese in particular) he is known as Kozah.

The Cult of Talos is led by the priestess [Fheralai Stormsworn](Fheralai%20Stormsworn.md). The cult’s headquarters are inside a death knight-dreadnaught, an undead battleship beached near a temple of Talos called the [Tower of Storms](Tower%20of%20Storms.md), which is guarded by [Moesko](Moesko.md), half-orc anchorite of Talos

The Anchorites of Talos are fervent religious cultists who worship of Talos, the god of storms.
