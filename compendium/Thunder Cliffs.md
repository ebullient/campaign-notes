# Thunder Cliffs
*Bluffs overlooking the Sea of Swords, [North Sword Coast](North%20Sword%20Coast.md)*  

The Thunder Cliffs were named by the people in the area for the sounds that the surf makes as it crashes into this high bluff overlooking the Sea of Swords.

* **Location** The Cliffs rest along the coastline halfway between the Mere of Dead Men and the city of Neverwinter.
