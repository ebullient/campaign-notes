## History

Lives deep in the west side of Neverwinter Wood. Fled to Thundertree, at first, to find Reidoth, but detected the dragon and changed his mind. He passed the party on his way south to Daran Edermath from Thundertree.

From [05. Finding a Cure](../writing/05.%20Finding%20a%20Cure.md)

> As they finished their meal (some campfire-roasted rabbit deftly collected by Immeral), there was a slight disturbance just outside the firelight. As the party gathered themselves for a fight, a centaur walked into their camp, hands outstretched to show he meant no harm. The centaur looked tired, and hungry. He introduced himself, and asked to share their campsite for the evening. The party agreed, and Xanth settled himself down, seeming to relax as he was surrounded by others. 

> Xanth was driven from his home in the wood by dangerous orcs, worse than the usual sort. They were tied up with spell casters, and a boar, and lightning. It was more than he could deal with on his own, and so he left his home. He would like to go back to free the forest from the danger, but doesn’t think he will find many that are willing to go. He was headed to Phandalin in the meanwhile. He knew that Daran Edermath, a friend he made long ago, would allow him to stay in his orchard while he sorted out how to recover his home.
