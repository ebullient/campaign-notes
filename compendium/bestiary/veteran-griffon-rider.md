> # Veteran Griffon Rider
>*Medium humanoid, neutral good*
>___
> - **Armor Class** 16 (chain mail)
> - **Hit Points** 105 (14d8 + 42)
> - **Speed** 30 ft.
>  
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|17 (+3)|10 (+0)|16 (+3)|10 (+0)|13 (+1)|12 (+1)|
> - **Saving Throws** Str +6, Con +6, Wis +4
> - **Skills** Perception +4
> - **Senses** passive Perception 14
> - **Languages** Common
> - **Challenge** 5 (1800 XP)
>___
> ***Indomitable (1/Day).*** The veteran can re-roll a saving throw that they fail.
> ## Actions
> ***Multiattack.*** The veteran makes three melee attacks.  
> ***Longsword.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. *Hit:* 8 (1d8 + 3) slashing damage.  
> ***Heavy crossbow.*** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +3 to hit, range 100/400 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d10) piercing damage.
> ## Reactions
> ***Parry.***  When an attacker hits with a melee attack, and the veteran can see the attacker, they can roll 1d6 and add the number rolled to their AC against the triggering attack, provided that they are wielding a melee weapon.
