# Yartar
<span class="subhead">Small city, Dessarin Valley, Savage Frontier</span>

Fortified city, situated on the Evermoor Way, on the eastern bank of the River Surbrin, overlooking the nearby River Dessarin. 

Member city of the Lords' Alliance lead by current Waterbaron, **Nestra Ruthiol**

* **Population** ~6000
* **Government** Baronry; Waterbaron is elected for life

Yartar has a thriving fishing community, builds barges. 

Important waystop between Silverymoon and Waterdeep. Many of its business cater to caravaneers, merchants and other Faerûnians that regularly travel the roads of the North.

Hosts large hiring fair in years without Shieldmeet.

Yartar is the historic rival of [Triboar](triboar.md) and animosity between the two communities is pervasive. While it is typically held in good nature, it has occasionally escalated to violence.

The Hand of Yartar is a fractious thieves' guild made up entirely of women.

The [Lionshield Coster](groups/lionshield-coster.md) is a trading company based in Yartar. Their symbol is a lion's head atop a field of blue

### Travel Distances from Yartar

* To Silverymoon - 260 miles (via the Evermoor Way)
* To Triboar - 60 miles (via the Evermoor Way)
* To Waterdeep - 340 miles (via the Evermoor Way and the Long Road)
