# Lionshield Coster
*Merchant company*  

The Lionshields are a merchant company based in Yartar (WAAY east). The company ships finished goods to Phandalin and other small settlements in the region.

* [Linene Graywind](../Phandalin/Linene%20Graywind.md) is the master of the trading post in Phandalin.
