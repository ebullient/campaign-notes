# Stone Cold Reavers
<span class="subhead">Mercenaries, neutral evil, Sword Coast North</span>

Neutral evil human veterans who speak Common.  
Sellswords (brigands, thieves)

## Details
<span class="subhead">Mercenary, [Veteran](../statblocks/veteran.md)</span>

### Syleen Wintermoon

* Leader
* Lean, brash woman 
* Long, red hair pulled back into a ponytail
* Lies compulsively, and assumes everyone else is lying
* She loves taking risks, living as though each day will be her last
* From Neverwinter

### Runa Vokdottir

* Muscular woman with cropped black hair, cold blue eyes
* Doesn't speak much
* Biting sense of humor
* Lifetime of sunless days and frigid nights: cruelty is natural, not evil.
* From Icewind Dale far to the north

### Brakkis Elspaar

* Unshapely man whose best years are behind him
* looks more like a cheerful innkeeper than he does a ruffian.
* Opportunist who likes following orders ? frees his mind to think about..
* **Retirement** and **dogs**

### Jabarl the Orc-Biter

* Rugged, sullen brute with criminal past (that has never caught up with him)
* Good at scaring people, caving in skulls, and tending horses.
(He seems most at ease around horses)

### Dobin Noreth

* Secretive: Mysterious man in a dark cloak
* Under cloak: pleasant in appearance, with copper hair and large gray eyes. 
* He wears leather armor and wields a short sword.
* Drives a hard bargain.
* Rides Four-Leaf Clover, a retired, well-behaved race horse.
