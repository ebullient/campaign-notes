### 2-6 Eleint: Downtime in Phandalin
6 days. Rest. Preparation.

You found Mabina, who was quite pleased with the Splendiferous Bauble. She will identify items for free, and did so for all of the unidentified items you were carrying.

Riardon quizzed villagers, which led you to Daran Edermath, who shared the history of a fortress high in the mountains that matched the vision from the Shrine of Savras. Also learned some things about dragons.

Orcs raided the village on the 4th! You made a friend by not killing one.

Grizzlebear met a tree. Opinions are divided on whether or not 'Knotty Dave' was funny, but the tree was happy. The tree also shared that Cryovain has made a few low-flights over the town late at night (as a tree, it fears deep freeze as much as it does fire. Seasonal changes are fine, but the cold of a dragon is something else).
