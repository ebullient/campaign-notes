# 20-21 Eliant, 1498: Discovery Expedition

<span class='ob-timelines' data-class='player' data-date='1498-09-20-00' data-title='Expedition: What happened? '></span>

Left Phandalin, nothing amiss on the road north.
After travelling west for a bit, you entered a copse of trees hiding a really strange land formation.. aka ambush site.

### Ambush
Ambush! Several goblins dead, one got away.
4 dead horses in the road: two with saddles, two with yokes/harness for a wagon.
(Oh! There is the wagon, too)

### Cave hideout
Followed Goblin tracks very stealthily.
Grizzlebear cast "water walk" -- no splashing.

Surprised 3 Goblins outside.. (they were burned in fire, and then shot with arrows)
Went inside. Bribed winter wolves with Gorthok meat.

A little farther North, found Goblins guarding the bridge:
- Riardon: Phantasmal Force! They think they are on fire
- Grizzlebear: Heat Metal -- one of them takes fire damage from armor.
Both guards run towards water (east)

Brocc stealthily made his way up the escarpment to the west, found the room full of Goblins. The rest of the party either climbed the wall or scrambled up the escarpment into the cave tunnel.

Fight w/ many Goblin bosses, with hostages in the way. Grizzlebear took advantage of wings to get between the leader and the hostages. 
Buh-bye to all the Goblins.

### Hostages
* Lionshield Caravan Guard: Jeremiah Mundu  
    Jeremiah kept struggling, and so spent much of the time knocked out. He doesn't know much about what happened after they were ambushed. 

* Sildar Hallwinter  
    Sildar was roughed up, but seemed to bear it better. He shared: 
    * Klarg, the bugbear who leads this goblin band, had orders to waylay large caravans headed to Phandalin.
    * Sildar needs to reach Phandalin, as there is someone there he is supposed to meet. He offered 500 gp to provide safe escort after they clear out the cave.
