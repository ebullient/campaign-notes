# 19 Eliant, 1498: Phandalin

<span class='ob-timelines' data-class='player' data-date='1498-09-19-00' data-title='Phandalin '>Caught up with Harbin. Search and rescue mission, it is.</span>

Talked to Harbin. Shared news. Harbin borrowed Fheralai's notes to make copies to send elsewhere.

Harbin laid the following situation at your feet: 

* A visitor that Harbin was expecting left Neverwinter 7 days ago and hasn't arrived. 
* This visitor was traveling alongside a Lionshield supply wagon, which is also missing.
* Dazlyn Grayshard was expecting a wagon full of mining supplies to be delivered to Barthen's Provisions.

Something bad has happened, or is happening. He doesn't know if this ended up as one ambushed caravan, or if there were two (or even three) separate events. He does know that they intended to follow the usual trade route (High Road to Triboar Trail).

Dazlyn is impatient to get started on a new site. He and Norbus are offering a 500gp reward for recovery of their missing mining supplies. Linene doesn't have enough time before Highharvestide to replace her supplies, either. She is posting a 500gp reward if her missing goods can be recovered.

He didn't say much about the visitor, but you know they should have been able to defend themselves. There would be some additional reward for rescue of this person, but Harbin did not specifiy a specific amount. Riardon got the impression that Harbin was worried that something he was excited about wouldn't happen if this person didn't arrive in Phandalin.
