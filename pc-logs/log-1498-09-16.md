# 16 Eleint, 1498: The Gulthias Tree  

[12-the-gulthias-tree](../pc-writing/12-the-gulthias-tree.md)

<span class='ob-timelines' data-class='player' data-date='1498-09-16-00' data-title='The Gulthias Tree'>Some digging, ropes, a mammoth. Timber!</span>

Breakfast, some loose plans.
Two-hour hike from Falcon's Lodge to the Manse

The tree looked.. whole? Not good, but much less damaged than expected. The Manse felt ominous, malevolent.

Some digging, and ropes, and a Mammoth!
Team effort resulted in one fully uprooted Gulthias tree.

The air cleared: the malevolent presence vanished, leaving only a sad old house behind.

Reidoth did some poking around (as he knew the wizard the house used to belong to), and he found the Staff of Birdcalls.

The party, Reidoth and Xanth returned to Falcon's Lodge.
Riardon played Lute of Illusions. Immeral accepted Boots of Elvenkind.
