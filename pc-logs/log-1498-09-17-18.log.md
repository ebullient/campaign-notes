# 17-18 Eleint, 1498 

<span class='ob-timelines' data-class='player' data-date='1498-09-17-00' data-title='Homeward Bound'>A bizarre game of fetch. Cloud wagons. Pixie tricks.</span>

You bid a fond(er) farewell to Falcon, and thanked him for his hospitality.

Walking through the forest, with the Ranger with favored terrain in the front and the high-perception Druid following behind, was uneventful, but between the edge of the forest and the road, a large Ettin played with his death-dog, "Bitey". 
You (collectively, and almost simultaneously) managed to find a new stick for the dog. Kyle used a lot of strength to hurl the new stick, which Bitey chased, dove after, pounced on, and then happily bounded away with. The Ettin was happy that his dog was happy, which left you free and clear to make your way to the road.

You camped a little after sundown, once the booming voice of the Ettin and Bitey's barking had faded into the distance.

[LONG REST]

You were visited by Pixies overnight. Some shoes were tied together, your hair was knotted, a pack strap was unstitched... Nothing was damaged beyond quick repair, and nothing was taken. You may or may not have heard a giggle while disentangling yourselves in the morning.

After breaking camp, you walked until the road to Phandalin split from the Triboar Trail, and stopped there for lunch. You had the weirdest experience as you were packing up: a misty/foggy cloud literally rolled right through you, turning the same corner, heading to Phandalin. 

Once you resumed walking, you noticed that a cloud had formed on the road in front of you. As you approached, that cloud solidified into Mabina's wagon. She greeted you warmly, surprised to meet you out on the road (she probably expected to run into you in town).
