Starting point: ***Falcon's Lodge***

Gathered for breakfast at Falcon's Lodge.

Made some rough plans. Travelled with large party including Reidoth and Xanth to Woodland Manse. The grounds were quiet. Deserted. Malevolent. The tree looks whole. Far more healthy than you expected, given the damage you did to it the last time you were here. 

There was some auick last minute planning, and then Reidoth summoned two earth elementals, who loosened the earth around the tree and the well. Then Kyle attatched the rope to the tree. Brocc was mammothed, and the rope was then hitched to him. Brocc gave a mighty heave, and the walls of the well split as the tree began to move in the direction it was pulled. 

Xanth suggested that Reidoth use stone shape to push the tree out of the ground, and (with haste and care), Reidoth was able to touch an unbroken bit of the well wall on the far side of the tree. The summoned earth elementals still lingered in the ground, and they helped guide and propel the tree using the stone shaped by Reidoth for that purpose. The tree flew up and over the courtyard wall, raining dirt and small stones everywhere. 

After the party left the courtyard to deal with the the remains of the tree, Reidoth thanked and dismissed the earth elementals before casting moonbeam on a patch of disturbed earth in the ruined courtyard. He used the next few minutes to systematically move the beam across all of the loose, overturned earth to burn any small roots that may have broken off.
