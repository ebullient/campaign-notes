[Afternoon]

Reidoth arrived, too late to help. Healed everyone and caused plants and vines to grow over the no-longer-standing stones. He collapsed the entrances to the anchorites caves, and filled those with growing plants, too.

Massive information exchange. Realized that controller of Blights, Grannoc, was killed. Decided to destroy the tree to halt the progress of Blights in the Wood.

All went to Falcon's Lodge and were allowed in. Even Reidoth. After much discussion, Falcon offered Boots of Elvenkind to the party.

Riardon identified all outstanding magic items.
