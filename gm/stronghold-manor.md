# Building the Manor
Rebuild Tresendar manor.
Two levels of dungeon/basement (with Nothic)
Completely ruined upstairs



## Notes from sources

### S&F
Repairing a ruin takes half of the time/supplies. 

### PHB
Pay at beginning of tenday or month. When at home, these would be folded into expenses.

| Lifestyle    | Price/day |
|--------------|-----|
| Modest       | 1gp |
| Comfortable  | 2gp |
| Wealthy      | 4gp |
| Aristocratic | 10gp min|

### DMG

#### Hirelings
Rarely important to story: coach driver, letter carrier/messenger, etc.

#### Recurring expenses for Property
Maintenance costs paid every 30 days. 

| Property               | Cost/day | Skilled | Untrained |
|------------------------|----------|---------|-----------|
| Keep or Small castle   | 100gp    | 50      | 50        |
| Hunting lodge          | 5sp      | 1       |           |
| Noble estate           | 10gp     | 3       | 15        |
| Palace or large castle | 400gp    | 200     | 100       | 

Staff should include a steward who can make payments in the party's absence.

#### Building a stronghold
 
 Character may need a royal charter, a land grant, or a deed. 
* A small estate might sell for as little as 100gp, or as much as 1000gp.
* A large estate might cost 5000gp or more, if it can be bought at all.

| Stronghold       | Cost    | Time     |
|------------------|---------|----------|
| Keep or Small Castle | 50000gp | 400 days |
| Noble estate with manor | 25000gp | 150 days |
| Palace or Large Castle | 500000gp | 1200 days |

Work can continue while the character is away, but each day the character is away adds 3 days to the construction time.
