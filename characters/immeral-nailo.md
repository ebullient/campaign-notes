## History

Immeral was born of a royal family, even though he could never participate in anything. 

Immeral was a small wood elf, he was overlooked by everyone except his family. He always thought he had to do something to prove himself. 

One day immeral got lost in a huge forest he was scared but also brave, there he met a pixie named Winnie, his only friend. When Immeral and Winnie were playing they stumbled across an Orc raid party coming towards the city/kingdom Immeral ran and ran 25 miles non-stop! 

Immeral warned his father of the raid and luckily it was stopped and there were no casualties thanks to immeral. Immeral was now one of the most respected people in the entire city (it was a small city). Immeral took himself as the protector of Barab (name of city). 

There was one attack Immeral could not stop, a very evil dragon decided to destroy Barab. While this was Happening Immeral was on a mission to deliver some gold to another town, the dragon ATTACKED! It was a total surprise; he and his minions raided the city killing and destroying everyone and everything. Immeral returned to find all of the buildings reduced to ash and bodies lying everywhere, scorched and dead.

Immeral saw his little sister curled up in a corner of a destroyed building, he saw a minion about to attack his sister. Immeral pulled out his short sword and attacked the beast and barely did anything. The monster turned around and laughed and said “You pathetic elf” and the creature shot fire at immeral. Immeral dodged it, but the moment it stopped the creature slashed immeral with claws out of fire. 

Immeral told his sister, “Run away and never come back! I will find you, and if I do not always remember,” The creature took another swing at Immeral but Immeral reflected it right back at him, “NEVER GIVE UP!” Immeral turned to find more monsters coming for him. Immeral killed all of them but now, he was alone with no one to turn to. 

Immeral was now an outlander.

Years and years later, after ages spent wandering, Immeral returned to the Sword Coast. While walking on the rough road leading to Phandalin, he heard a giggle near his ear, and a tiny Pixie appeared. She looked just like Winnie, but .. younger, almost too young to be away from her village. “Are you an adventurer? I want to go on an adventure, too.” She tilted her head, “You look just like my great-grandma’s best friend,” she said. “You disappeared ages ago. Where have you been?”
