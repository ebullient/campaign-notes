## 2. background

The *inciting incident* of this campaign is when Fheralai Stormsworn, the high priestess of Talos from the Storm Lord's Wrath and other expansion quests, rouses the half-orc anchorites of Neverwinter Wood. Telling these acolytes of Talos that she has been given a mission by their evil god of storms, and it’s time to start shaking up the Sword Coast! Although Fheralai’s not actually in this beginner campaign, so your big bad isn't in danger of being killed too early :)

——

Following Fheralai’s orders, the half-orc Moesko takes over the Tower of Storms, and this location remains pretty much as written in Icespire Peak, except the banshee, Miraal, is combined with Agatha, the banshee from Lost Mine because her wisdom is sought by several NPCs, so the party will have more of a reason to go to this tower and help her overcome Moesko.

When Cryovain flees from Thundertree to Icespire Hold, they stir up the mountain orcs from that fortress and from Wyvern Tor, leaving that cave empty. These displaced orcs take over Butterskull Ranch, as written in Icespire Peak, except it is the named leader of the Many Arrows orc tribe, Brughor Axe-Biter from Lost Mine, who steals and wears Big Al’s ten gallon cowboy hat (trust me, that’s an extremely important detail). The orcs also go to the Woodland Manse, AND they displace the Whiskered Gang wererats from the Shrine of Savras, as written.

When these wererats move into the Mountain’s Toe Gold Mine, they force out the Stone Cold Reavers (that hardly-mentioned bandit gang in Icespire Peak). Some of the Reavers follow Cryovain to Icespire Hold, as written, but a larger group of them join the ranks of Iarno “Glasstaff” Albrek, the evil wizard from Lost Mine -- whose character can be merged with the Black Spider and the random necromancer from the Old Owl Well who is a mysterious Red Wizard of Thay; therefore, he calls his bandits “Redbrands!” It’s ALL connected!

Then, as written in Lost Mine, the Redbrands pretty much take over Phandalin and start manipulating the Cragmaw Goblins to ambush anyone travelling into town. Including Gundren Rockseeker AND Don Jon Raskin who are both travelling there from Neverwinter, and who have hired the party to bring their supplies after them from Neverwinter to Phandalin to reopen the Mountain’s Toe Gold Mine.
