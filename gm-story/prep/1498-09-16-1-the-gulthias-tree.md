* Immeral: Elf Ranger. Last of tribe (Sister?). Favored terrain: forest. Favored enemy: Dragon, Orc, Goblin

* The Courtyard & The Tree

> It was only 3 days ago, right? You were here only 3 days ago? Maybe the anchorite was controlling the growth of the tree in addition to the blights, because the tree looks whole. Far more healthy than you expected, given the damage you did to it the last time you were here. Its blackened, twisted limbs reach upward, like a skeletal hand clawing its way out of the earth. Evil seems to pulse from it, like a heartbeat.
> What you didn't notice fully the last time you were here, however, is that the tree doesn't grow out of the ground. It's trunk, which looks like some deathly interpretation of a mangrove tree, emerges from the ground through the opening of an abandoned well.

Roll initiative (just for an order)

**Skill Challenge:**  Your task is to *completely uproot* the Gulthias Tree.

You are free to try whatever you feel might work. I will now give you a few minutes, now that you you know who is going in what order, to come up with a plan that best uses everyone's skills to uproot the tree (remember you have Xanth and Reidoth to draw from as well).

You need 6 successful checks between all of you to fully uproot the tree before 3 failures.


***Success*** 

The Gulthias tree is completely uprooted, and the ominous bite to the air starts to dissipate.

***Failure***

There is a pause, and while the air seems less ominous for the moment, you know that you haven't been successful. The tree will regrow. Someone will have to come back and deal with the regrown tree another time.
